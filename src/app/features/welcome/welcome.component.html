Welcome to genese-api-angular demonstration module.

With genese cli, you generated automatically your data-services and your datatypes with the command line

genese new api.

Now, this app will show you how to use the data-services of genese-angular with your data-services.

At first, don't forget to launch the NestJs backend : open another terminal, move on the directory /backend, and type

npm run start

Open the console of your navigator : the results of the requests will be displayed inside.

Now, you're ready to test the genese-angular methods ! Click on one button on the left.
